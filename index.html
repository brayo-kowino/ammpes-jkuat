About Us

The Association of Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering Students (AMMPES) is a vibrant, student-driven community dedicated to advancing excellence, innovation, and sustainability in the fields of mining and mineral processing.

We create opportunities for collaboration, leadership development, and industry engagement through mentorships, technical events, and community projects empowering tomorrow’s engineers to shape a smarter, cleaner, and sustainable future.

Meet the Team

Mission & Vision

We are driven by purpose to shape the next generation of student engineers through innovation, sustainability, and leadership.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant and inclusive environment where students explore innovation, sustainability, and professionalism in mining and mineral processing through academic and industrial engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading student body that nurtures skilled, ethical, and future-ready engineers transforming the mining industry sustainably for generations to come.

Our Core Values

The foundation of everything we do guiding our actions, mindset, and purpose as future mining engineers.

Integrity

We uphold honesty and strong moral principles in all our engagements.

Innovation

We nurture creative thinking to solve challenges in mining and technology.

Excellence

We pursue high standards in academics, events, and leadership.

Sustainability

We promote responsible mining that safeguards the planet’s future.

Collaboration

We encourage teamwork and unity within and beyond our community.

Leadership

We empower students to become confident and ethical leaders.

What We Do

Academic Support

We run mentorships, academic clinics, and knowledge-sharing forums to empower academic success.

Industrial Exposure

Through field visits, industry talks, and hands-on workshops, we prepare students for real-world applications.

Leadership & Networking

We develop leadership capacity and link students with professionals, alumni, and mentors.

Community Engagement

We support outreach programs, conservation initiatives, and social impact projects.
