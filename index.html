Academic Field Trip

Destination: Space and Style Limited, Juja

Dear MMPE Students,

I am pleased to invite you all for an industrial visit to Space and Style Limited. This is a valuable opportunity to witness large-scale manufacturing and materials application firsthand.

Date & Time

Wed, 25th March 2026

08:00 AM (Sharp)

Meeting Point

Outside Eng. Lab Bldg

JKUAT Main Campus

Academic Requirement: Please carry writing materials and be ready to engage with the technical team. You will be required to write and submit a comprehensive report after the trip.

Safety Compliance (Mandatory)

Access to the facility is strictly dependent on your safety gear. You must bring and wear your Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) including your Overall and Safety Boots.

* Students without the required PPE will NOT be allowed to participate.

Ms. Fatuma Rajab

About Us

A distinguished platform for engineering excellence.

The Association of Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering Students (AMMPES) is a distinguished organization focused on promoting and advancing the field of mining engineering in Kenya.

We provide a dynamic platform for knowledge exchange, advocacy, and innovative solutions, empowering our members in their professional growth while contributing to the sustainable development of the mining industry.
