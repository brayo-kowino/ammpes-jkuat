About Us

The Association of Mining and Mineral Processing Engineering Students (AMMPES) is a dynamic student-led body committed to fostering academic excellence, professional growth, and innovation in the fields of mining and mineral processing. We serve as a platform for collaboration, leadership development, industry engagement, and advocacy for sustainable engineering practices. Through technical forums, mentorship, industrial exposure, and community initiatives, AMMPES empowers the next generation of mining engineers to lead with purpose and impact.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant and inclusive environment for students to explore innovation, sustainability, and professionalism in mining and mineral processing through academic and industrial engagement.

Our Vision

To be a leading student body that nurtures skilled, ethical, and future-ready engineers transforming the mining industry sustainably.
